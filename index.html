[INSERT EXPLAINIATION] From my understanding, before reseaarching my intuituion is that the distortion is due to the angle at which light rays from the face will hit the camera. From close by, the ray paths from the face to the lens would be much more acute while from far the rays follow a gentler, almost parallel slope. I think that this would cause distortion because in the letter case, things that are equal distance apart will have thier light rays reach the camera roughly equal distance apart since the rays will be near parallel, but in the latter case the ray path becomes more and more inclined as you move away from the center of the camera causing discrepancies between actual distance between things, and distance between the lightrays when they reach the lens.
